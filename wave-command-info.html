Wave Bot Documentation

Wave is a all-in-one bot with a bunch of useful features, wich every server owner needs! It includes moderation, fun, rp and utility commands!

 

Commands as of 2020-07-30:

 

𝗠𝗼𝗱𝗲𝗿𝗮𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻 𝗖𝗼𝗺𝗺𝗮𝗻𝗱𝘀:

?ban <@user>
Bans the desired user.
?kick <@user> Kicks the desired user.
?warn <@user> (reason)
Warns the desired user in their Direct Messages.
?clear (number)
Deletes the desired amount of messages.
?userinfo <@user or UserID>
Shows useful information about the desired user.

 

𝗙𝘂𝗻 𝗰𝗼𝗺𝗺𝗮𝗻𝗱𝘀:

?waifu
Shows a waifu!
?meme
Shows a meme from r/memes!
?8ball <Question>
Answers a question.
?animememe
Shows an anime meme from r/AnimeMemes!
?slot
Roll a slot machine.
?howhot <@user>
Returns the percent of how hot is a user.
?reverse <text>
Reverse a text!

 

𝗨𝘁𝗶𝗹𝗶𝘁𝘆 𝗖𝗼𝗺𝗺𝗮𝗻𝗱𝘀:

?ping Shows the bot's latency (ping)
?say <message> Makes the bot say the desired message.
?credits
Shows the owner's social media and credits.
?createchannel <name>
Creates a text channel with the desired name.
?createvc <name>
 Creates a voice channel with the desired name.
?serverinfo
 Displays useful information about the server.
?giveaway
 Creates a giveaway!
?translate <text>
Translates a text to english.
?invite
Shows the invite link for the bot.
